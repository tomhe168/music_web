AudioToMiDi Magic

A groundbreaking feature that effortlessly transforms your audio files into MIDI format. Our innovative technology identifies the distinct musical elements within your audio tracks, such as pitch, rhythm, and instrumentation, and accurately converts them into MIDI data. This enables you to edit, manipulate, and recreate your music with unprecedented flexibility and precision.

To deliver an exceptional audio experience and accommodate a wide range of music styles, we currently prioritize support for four of the most popular instruments
Guitar, Drums, Piano, and Bass. 
Moving forward, we will continuously expand the variety of supported instruments and further enhance the performance of each instrument type.

Instrument Swapper

InstrumentSwapper allows you to effortlessly switch the instrument in your music while maintaining the original melody and rhythm.
This feature brings versatility and freshness to your musical creations by enabling new instrument combinations.
